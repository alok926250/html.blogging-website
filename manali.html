Unforgettable Manali Hill Station

The beauty, weather, and culture of Manali are totally different and unique from other hill stations. I have traveled to many other hill stations in India with my friends and family, but when I visited Manali for the first time in June 2010, I decided that Manali is the best. Before my visit, people used to say that Manali is the Switzerland of India, and I have found that this is totally true.



Adventure Sports Abound

During my visit, I found that Manali features a lot of adventure sports, like river crossing, valley crossing, bungee jumping, and adventure bridge climbing in Club House Manali and Van Vihar, as well as whitewater rafting in Beas River in Kullu. There's also zorbing, hot air ballooning, and mountain biking in Solang Valley, just 15 km from Mall Road.



Pleasant Summer Weather

The weather was always pleasant during our June visit, and it increased our appetite both for having fun and eating. My daughter, my wife, and I spent only two nights and three days in this beautiful place, and we felt close to nature and adventure at the same time. All the recreational activities we did in those three days refreshed us, and we felt more active at the end of the trip than we had before. I wish to return to this unforgettable place in the wintertime—sometime in December—to see it covered in ice.
